Nigar Khana-e-Ghazal

Written by Wali RizviJanuary 16, 2016January 16, 2016 1 Comment

Baquer Zaidi’s newest book “Nigar Khana-e-Ghazal” has been published and is now available online at /books/nigar.pdf

باقِرِؔ زیدی جسے کہتے ہیں اربابِ سخن
آبشارِ شاعری کہسارِ امریکہ میں ہے
آیت اللہ عقیلؔ الغروی

It is a collection of Baquer Zaidi’s Ghazals grouped into three parts, “Nigar-e-Ghazal”, “Dar-e-Ghazal” and “Diyar-e-Ghazal”
